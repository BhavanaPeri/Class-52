#1. Decimal number System

-Based on the power of 10
-0 to 9 digits and total 10 digits

ex: 231 = 200 + 30 + 1
        = 2 * 100 + 3 * 10 + 1 * 1 
        = 2 * 10 ^ 2 + 3 * 10 ^ 1 + 1 * 10 ^ 0

#2. Binary Number System

-Based on the power of 2
-0 to 1 digits and in total 2 digits

ex: 101 = 1 * 2 ^ 2 + 0 * 2 ^ 1 + 1 * 2 ^ 0
        = 1 * 4 + 0 * 2 + 1 * 1
        = 4 + 0 + 1
        = 5

#3. Hexadecimal number system

-Based on the power of 16
-0 to 9 digits and A to F digits
-In total 15 digits
-A:10
-B:11
-C:12
-D:13
-E:14
-F:15

ex: AD = A * 16 ^ 1 + D * 10 ^ 0
       = 10 * 16 + 13 * 0
       = 160 + 0
       = 160

    FF = F * 16 ^ 1 + F * 16 ^ 0 
        = 15 * 16 + 15 * 1
        = 240 + 15
        = 255 

    80 = 8 * 16 ^ 1 + 0 * 16 ^ 0
        = 8 * 16 + 0 * 1
        =  128 + 0
        = 128 
        
  00 = 0 * 16 + 0 * 1 
     = 0 * 16 ^ 1 + 0 * 16 ^ 0
     = 0
